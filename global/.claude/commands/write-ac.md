Invoke the write-ac skill. Transforms an authored PRD (PRD-NNN functional stories) and ORD (ORD-NNN operational requirements, KPPs tagged) into testable Jira acceptance criteria positioned at the right altitude — KPPs and headline outcomes promote to Capability-level AC, detailed story criteria flow to child Epics/Stories. Runs in two phases: Phase 1 (AFK) reads the PRD/ORD/cross-link matrix, resolves the linked Jira Capability, and presents an altitude split for confirmation; Phase 2 (HITL) writes docs/ac/[capability]-AC.md carrying PRD-NNN/ORD-NNN traceability into each AC, then optionally pushes to the linked Jira Capability via the jira MCP behind a typed PUSH gate. Handles PRD-only or ORD-only inputs. Consumes requirements — never authors them. Use when you have a PRD and/or ORD ready to turn into Jira acceptance criteria, or are promoting a project to a Jira Capability.
