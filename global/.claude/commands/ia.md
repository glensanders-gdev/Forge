Invoke the ia skill. Runs an impact assessment for a proposed change — conducts a full grill-with-docs session to sharpen the change, then searches global and company knowledge, ADRs, RAID log, and backlog to produce a severity-tagged impact summary, an adaptive change brief or PRD, and draft artefacts for any affected files. All output is written to docs/ia/IA-YYYYMMDD-NNNN/ and is isolated from the live knowledge structure. Use when proposing a change to a knowledge file, process, or system and you need to understand the blast radius before committing.
