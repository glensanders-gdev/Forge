Invoke the front-gate skill. Structured intake for non-technical users submitting an idea or request for team consideration. Interprets the idea in plain language, checks relevant system knowledge and contractual obligations, then grills the requestor one question at a time to produce a complete Request Brief covering: problem statement, objective, metrics (optional), team ask, risk of inaction, negative impacts, and brief summary. Output saved to docs/requests/YYYY-MM-DD-[slug].md. Use when a stakeholder or non-technical user wants to formally submit an idea or request.
